Our Story

Founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf, Rolex has stood as the ultimate pioneer in swiss luxury watchmaking. Driven by a relentless quest for excellence, our mechanical ateliers in Geneva continue to perfect the horological arts, integrating micro-precision gears with premium materials such as 18-carat gold and high-grade oystersteel.

Every timepiece represents months of rigorous assembly, hand polishing, and calibration inside state-of-the-art laboratory modules. Rolex watchmakers assemble complicated chronographs and perpetual motions capable of running reliably under extreme atmospheric pressure—from the deepest ocean ravines to the highest mountain summits.

Choosing a luxury watch is more than acquiring a method of keeping time; it is secure investment in art, a marker of profound achievements, and a physical legacy of precision designed to endure across multiple generations.

Micro Precision

Each balance wheel and perpetual self-winding movement is calibrated to standard margins of less than two seconds deviation per day.

Excellence

Only the finest diamonds, platinum alloys, 18k rose gold, and deep forest-green dials earn a placement in our watches.

Heritage

Preserving classical Swiss engineering traditions while leading state-of-the-art material patents.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or make a purchase from this luxury watch e-commerce platform.

1. Personal Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact our concierge, we collect specific personal information from you, including your name, shipping address, email address, phone number, and encrypted payment details. We secure this data inside private physical storage vaults with military-grade encryption keys.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the order data we collect exclusively to fulfill orders placed through our secure checkout system (including processing your credit card payment, arranging for armored courier shipping, and providing you with receipt confirmations).

3. Data Retention

When you complete an transaction through the Site, we will maintain your Order Records for our tax and historic compliance archives unless you explicitly request us to purge this information from our secure systems.

Return Policy

We provide a comprehensive, fully-insured 30-day return policy for all unworn luxury watches in our catalog.

1. Condition Criteria

The timepiece must be returned in pristine, brand-new condition. All security tags, clear protective plastic covers on sapphire crystals, presentation leather cases, user manuals, and chronometer certifications must remain completely intact and unaltered.

2. Armored Return Shipping

For high-value assets, standard postal options are strictly forbidden. Upon request, our concierge will schedule a certified armored courier collection team to pick up the watch directly from your residence at our expense. This guarantees absolute safety.

3. Verification & Refund

Once returned items are received at our main ateliers in Geneva, our master watchmakers will perform a microscopic multi-point verification to confirm caliber authenticity. Refunds are credited within 5 business days upon successful clearance.

Shipping Information

We arrange highly secure, fully-insured logistics channels to deliver your horological acquisitions safely.

1. Rates & Free Dispatch

For acquisitions with a total value exceeding $10,000, we provide complimentary insured overnight armored courier delivery. For orders under $10,000, we charge a flat premium delivery fee of $150 to support armed courier tracking, specialized signature controls, and full transit insurance.

2. Delivery Protocol

Due to the high-value nature of luxury Swiss timepieces, we do not support delivery to PO Boxes, unmanned lockers, or general drop sites. All deliveries require physical presence, direct identity verification with a state-issued passport or license, and a direct handwritten signature from the registered cardholder.

3. International Customs

We serve over 180 countries worldwide. International shipments dispatch from Geneva, Switzerland with pre-cleared custom declarations. Any local import taxes or duties are calculated at checkout to ensure seamless delivery.
